Why Course Correct?

Exam class drills

We offer special classes to students writing a variety of exams, namely GCSE, 11+ exams, SAT, Leaving Certificate, WJEC and N5. They are given a high priority and are drilled in order to meet the standard of the various exam boards. We work closely with experienced personel on these boards to ensure the right material and up to date syllabus are being taught to your child.

Homework and school help

For busy parents, course correct is the perfect solution to help your children with homework and school work. In addition to following school curriculum and helping your children stay on top of school work, we give them even more work to keep them occupied on free days and strengthen understanding in various concepts being taught.

Progress Reports

At course correct, no child leaves us the same way they came. Our drills and classes are designed to be tailored to the special need of each child as we know learning styles and rates differ; this facilitates increased understanding and comprehension in the subject. We give progress reports at the end of each month and also make sure to communicate with parents regularly to ensure learning goals are being met.

Quality control

Course correct goes the extra mile to ensure quality, seasoned tutors with relevant experience are hired. All our tutors are finely hand-picked. Your child will be learning from the best of the best!
